In keeping with a more compassionate perspective, early definitions of health
focused narrowly on the body's ability to function. But the meaning of health
has evolved over time, expanding to include the mind, the heart, and the spirit.

## Health as harmony

True wellbeing is not merely the absence of illness. It is a quiet harmony — a
sense that the different parts of our life are moving in the same direction.
When the body is rested, the mind is clear, and the heart is open, we feel
*whole*.

## Small, sustainable seeds

You do not need a dramatic transformation. You need small, repeatable acts of
care:

1. Drink water before coffee.
2. Step outside for ten minutes of daylight.
3. Move your body in a way that feels like joy, not punishment.
4. Protect your sleep as if it were sacred — because it is.

Health is not a destination you arrive at and then forget. It is a relationship
you tend to, gently, every single day.
